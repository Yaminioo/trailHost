APPLICATIONS

AGRIBOT

Agribot is a solution to various farming problems. Agribot is tested by IARI, PAU, HAU, and many corporations as the best suited agricultural Drone for spraying over all types of crops and farmlands. To make Technically most advanced yet easy to use Drones for applications like Agriculture, Survey, Surveillance, Logistics and make it available in all parts of South-East Asia. Our facilities includes :- Dealer & Distributor network Pan India. Selling to multiple Customers - Farmers, Corporate customers & Institutions. India's 1st DGCA approved multipurpose advanced drone. Multi purpose drone spray/broadcast/crop health analysis. Autonomous drone - spray 1 acres in 6 minutes. Battery flight time-20 minutes(1 hectare spray).

HEAVYBOT

Heavybot is a customizable drone with a Coaxial octocopter, Hexacopter, Hybrid VTOL, and even a single rotor helicopter drone. The primary function of Heavybot is Logistics, and it is designed to move goods. To use a Heavybot, a user has to follow the guidelines of DGCA or need special permission from the Government for a particular purpose.

SURVEYBOT

Surveybot is a drone designed for aerial surveys with sensors, accuracy, and Terrain Compatibility. It has in-built sensors for secured drone monitoring and accurate data collection. The primary function of a Surveybot is to fly over the land for data collection and processing. It provides highly defined images with its sensors.

SWITCH All Terrain Dominator

SWITCH UAV is a first of its kind VTOL and fixed wing hybrid Unmanned Aerial Vehicle. SWITCH features advanced flight time, higher safety and simple operation with additional fail-safe redundancies. It is used for long duration operations, long endurance surveillance and security, inspection and photogrammetry Endurance: > 120 minutes

RYNOBOT

RYNO is a micro category survey-grade drone designed and specialized for mapping applications. It is equipped with an advanced mapping payload and state-of-the-art- PPK module to deliver superior results. Due to its high accuracy in mapping applications, it is the only micro category drone that has been approved by the Survey of India (SOI) towards the Swamitva Yojana, which is set to be one of the largest drone-powered mapping projects in the world.
